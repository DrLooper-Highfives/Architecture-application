Mental calcul

How playing ?

  • At the beginning of the game, you are selected at the addition and substraction with 2 digit item
  • You can change the ite
  • Clic on the "New game" button
  • In the first gray frame, an operation appears
  • Enter your result in the 2nd gray frame and validate it with the "Return" key.
  • If your result is good, score will adds with the number of following good answer you succeded (For nstance, if you just successed with the 3w followin good answer, your score will add 3 pts)
  • If your result is bad, the score will be inferred from 1 pt, except if you have 0 pt. More, the next adds will only be of 1 pt.
  • You have 2 mn to perform.
  • At the end of the time, the screen will change and will offer you to play another game. Final score will stay and the and a note on 20 (French type) is displayed.
Everybody can play this game. There is many levels. Primary school (addition and soustration tables), highschools pupils, or adults.
At end of each game, a note on 20 (french type quotation) is displayed, giving you your level.A chaque fin de partie, une note sur 20 est donnée vous donnant votre niveau. The 20 quotation can be obtained if you're training often.
